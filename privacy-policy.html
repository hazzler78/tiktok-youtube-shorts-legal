Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • API Credentials: Client keys, secrets, and tokens necessary to connect to YouTube and TikTok APIs
  • Content Data: Videos, titles, descriptions, and metadata you upload through the Service
  • Usage Data: Analytics and performance metrics from your posted content
  • Account Information: Channel IDs and platform account identifiers

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide the core functionality of cross-posting videos to YouTube and TikTok
  • Generate AI-powered captions and descriptions
  • Track analytics and performance metrics
  • Schedule and manage your content posts
  • Improve the Service and troubleshoot issues

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device or server where you host the application. We do not store your API credentials or content on external servers unless you explicitly configure cloud storage.

4. Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with:

  • YouTube API: Your content and credentials are transmitted to YouTube according to Google's Privacy Policy
  • TikTok API: Your content and credentials are transmitted to TikTok according to TikTok's Privacy Policy
  • xAI/Grok API: Content metadata may be sent to xAI for caption generation

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

  • API credentials are stored in environment variables, not in code
  • Database files are stored locally and should be secured by you
  • We use secure connections (HTTPS) when communicating with APIs

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data
  • Delete your data from the Service
  • Revoke API access at any time
  • Request information about how your data is used

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide the Service. You can delete your data at any time by removing database files and revoking API access.

8. Cookies and Tracking

The Service does not use cookies or tracking technologies. Any cookies used are from third-party APIs (YouTube, TikTok) and are subject to their respective privacy policies.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

11. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the application's support channels.
